Different device

Accesing a site with an Apple product could throw higher prices than other brands.

Different browser

Access a site with different browsers. Safari could show higher prices than Chrome.

Incognito mode

Access a site after clearing the cache or in incognito mode so you can enter and leave without cookies, so they won't learn your shopping patterns.

Banner ads

After accessing a site some banner ads with cool offers might "follow" you from site to site, you're being retargetted with AdSense.

Coupon codes

Search for coupon codes from affiliates online. I've used this for hosting services ;)
